Frequenty Asked Questions

Is there a prize money ?

A unique experience will be your reward. Of course, bragging rights go along with the turf!

What hydration support will be provided ?

We aim to make your experience custom. Along with providing the usual energy drinks, fruits and other fare, we’ll be happy to hold any specific items you’d like to drop off with us for consumption at any aid station. Yes, if you drop off biryani to eat at kilometer 30, we’ll hand it to you at the nearest aid station.

Is my registration money refundable ?

Fortunately for you, we are prepping for the event weeks/months in advance. So, just like you, your registration money is already hard at work prepping for the event. As such, refunds won’t be feasible.

Will there be a drop-bag facility ?

Definitely, in your car at the start/end point. More importantly, we offer a unique ‘drop-off’ facility for your hydration/nutrition needs at our aid stations.
